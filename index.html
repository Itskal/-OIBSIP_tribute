SERENA WILLIAMS

[September 26, 1981]

"It's so important to give your all no matter what you do,no matter how many obstacles you face"

Serena Williams is a retired American professional tennis player, widely regarded as one of the greatest athletes in the sport's history. Born on September 26, 1981, in Saginaw, Michigan, she began playing tennis at a young age and turned professional in 1995. Williams has won 23 Grand Slam singles titles, the most by any player in the Open Era, and has held the world No. 1 ranking for a total of 319 weeks. Known for her powerful serve and aggressive playing style, Williams has inspired many with her achievements both on and off the court. Beyond tennis, she is an advocate for gender equality and social justice, and she has launched various business ventures, including a fashion line. After her final competitive season in 2022, Williams continues to influence the sport and promote various causes.
